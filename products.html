EcoCrete

EcoCrete is an Apeo free emulsion specifically developed for compatibility with cements, especially Portland type cements.

The principal advantages of EcoCrete are enhanced adhesion and resilience, leading to improved impact resistance of the cement. The secondary advantages of EcoCrete is its ability to absorb unwanted moisture in concrete and cement surfaces and act as a base layer waterproofing barrier.t.
