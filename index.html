EcoCoat

EcoCoat (ecoc100) is a 2-in-1 product which means that it is a waterproofing and a paint. As a result of this you save time on application and the cost of labour by only doing it once!

View Details
EcoCrete

EcoCrete is an Apeo free emulsion specifically developed for compatibility with cements, especially Portland type cements. The principal advantages of EcoCrete are enhanced adhesion and resilience, leading to improved impact resistance of the cement.

View Details
EcoProof

EcoProof Heavy Duty is a seamless spray-applied protective coating designed to provide a heavy duty protection for slabs, metal roofs, retaining walls, boundary walls, foundations, ponds and dams.
